The Best Movies According to Asong

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Casino Royale

Starring: Daniel Craig, Eva Green, & Mads Mikklesen
Directed by: Martin Campbell
Genre: Action, Spy Thiller

One of the best Bond films of all-time. It has everything that you'd want in Spy film and Daniel craig doesn't disappoint with his first film as Bond.

The Batman

Starring: Robert Pattinson, Zoë Kravitz, & Paul Dano
Directed by: Matt Reeves
Genre: Action, Superhero

Robert Pattinson takes a new angle to who Bruce Wayne is at the start of his career as Batman. In addition, Zoë Kravitz shines as the new catwoman and Paul Dano is scaringly good as the Ridller.

Blade Runner 2049

Starring: Ryan Gosling, Harrison Ford, Ana De Armas, Sylvia Hoeks, & Jared Leto
Directed by: Denis Villeneuve
Genre: Neo-noir science fiction

Sequel to the 1982 hit Blade Runner, Blade runner 2049 is one of the most underrated science-fiction movies of all time. With the star-studded cast consisting of Ryan Gosling, Harrison Ford, and Ana de Armas, this long-awaited sequel does everything BUT disappoint.
